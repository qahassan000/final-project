Change in Video Game Sales Across the Years

The dataset used is Video Game Sales by Anand Shaw, it contains the following attributes: Rank, Name, Platform, Year, Genre, Publisher,
NA_Sales (in millions), EU_Sales (in millions), JP_Sales (in millions), Other_Sales (in millions), Global_Sales (in millions).

The visualization utilizes Year, Genre, and the different sales types to display the change in video game sales across genres throughout the years.
